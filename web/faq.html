When should test get invovled?

    Test should be involved in your IPT from the beginning. When you are forming your COAs for the XR and TRB boards, test should be there. As you are forming your requirements, test will help you review them to ensure they are testable. At the same time, know that your POCs will start formulating their thoughts on how to test your requirements. After your COA has been decided (Part III-V of the 1067) and your requirements are known, your test POC will help you hold a Modification Test Team Meeting. The Modification Test Team is detailed in the Integrated Test Team (ITT) Charter. The Modification Test Team includes you, ACC, 46TS, 605TES, AFOTEC, Program Office Test and others.

What is a MTT?

    The Modification Test Team (MTT) will help define the amount of testing, the test objectives, and a general testing strategy/method for your project. Because you are a part of this MTT, you will have the approval of the organizations when you present this strategy to leadership and the CCB. We will work from the same page from the beginning of your project/program even before you begin your development.

    In the past every project was mandated to have a DT. This was defined in policy letter #3. This policy letter was rewritten to have the Modification Test Team present a recommended Testing Strategy, which may or may not include DT. This new policy is sitting at the Executive Committee and may/may not be signed eventually. If it is signed their will be no more DT/OT waivers.
